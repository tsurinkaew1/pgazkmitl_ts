Master's and Ph.D. Positions Available in PSDSC Laboratory


We are excited to announce opportunities for motivated students to join our PSDSC Laboratory for advanced research in cutting-edge areas of power systems and distributed systems control.


Research Areas

  • Grid stability with distributed energy resources
  • Intelligent frameworks for distributed controllable loads
  • Renewable energy integration
  • Advanced control strategies for modern power systems
  • Developing open-source software

Who Should Apply?

  • Students passionate about power systems, control systems, or renewable energy
  • Background in Electrical Engineering, Energy Systems, or a related field
  • Strong analytical and programming skills (MATLAB, Python, or equivalent)

Position Details

  • Full-time Master's and Ph.D. opportunities
  • Competitive funding for tuition and living expenses
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities and mentoring from renowned faculty

Application Deadline

Open until filled


How to Apply

Send your CV, academic transcripts, and a brief statement of research interests to: tossaporn.su@kmitl.ac.th or issarachai.ng@kmitl.ac.th
