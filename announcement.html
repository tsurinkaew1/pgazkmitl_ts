Bachelor's, Master's, Ph.D., and Research Assistant Positions in PSDSC Research Center

We are excited to announce opportunities for motivated students to join our PSDSC Research Center for advanced research in cutting-edge areas of power system dynamics, stability, and control.


Position Details

  • Senior/junior project
  • Research Assistant
  • Full-time Master's and Ph.D. opportunities
  • Part-time Master's and Ph.D. opportunities

Research Areas

  • Power system dynamics, stability, and control of modern power systems and microgrids

Who Should Apply?

  • Students or researchers passionate about
    • Grid stability with distributed energy resources
    • Intelligent AI-based frameworks for distributed controllable resources
    • Data-driven control for inverter-based resources
    • Renewable energy integration
    • Harmonic reduction techniques in inverter-based resources
    • Monitoring and advanced control strategies for modern power systems
    • Developing open-source software for analyzing dynamics and stability of modern power grids.
  • Require background in Electrical Engineering, Power Systems, Energy Systems, or a related field
  • Require strong analytical and programming skills (MATLAB, Python, or equivalent)
