Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. Positions in PSDSC Laboratory

We are excited to announce opportunities for motivated students to join our PSDSC Laboratory for advanced research in cutting-edge areas of power systems and distributed systems control.


Research Areas

  • Power system dynamics, stability, and control of modern power systems and microgrids

Who Should Apply?

  • Students passionate about
    • Grid stability with distributed energy resources
    • Intelligent frameworks for distributed controllable
    • Renewable energy integration
    • Advanced control strategies for modern power system
    • Developing open-source software
  • Background in Electrical Engineering, Energy Systems, or a related field
  • Strong analytical and programming skills (MATLAB, Python, or equivalent)

Position Details

  • Senior project
  • Full-time Master's and Ph.D. opportunities
  • For Master degree and Ph.D., you may further discuss the availability of full funding for tuition and living expenses

Application Deadline

  • Open until filled

How to Apply

  • Send your CV, academic transcripts, and a brief statement of research interests to:
    • Dr. Tossaporn Surinkaew: tossaporn.su@kmitl.ac.th
    • Professor Dr. Issarachai Ngamroo: issarachai.ng@kmitl.ac.th
